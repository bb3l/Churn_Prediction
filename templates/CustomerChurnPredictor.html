In order to predict if a customer will churn in the near future, we will define the following features to be used for our model training:

- Credit score (range from 300-850)

- Gender (Male = 0, Female =1)

- Age

- Tenure(How long they have been with the bank)

- Account Balance (Amount of money in their account at the time)

- Number of Products (number of products they own with the bank)

- Has a credit card? (if the customer has a credit card or not, No=-0, Yes=1)

- Active member? (if the customer is an active member or not, No=0, Yes=1)

- Estimated Salary (How much the customer makes at the time)
